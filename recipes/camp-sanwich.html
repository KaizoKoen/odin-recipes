Campfire Breakfast Sandwich

Description

Make an egg breakfast sandwich over the campfire with your Hobo/Sandwich/Mountain Pie toaster.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter, soft
  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 slice Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the Hobo Pie toaster over the hot coals of a campfire, 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Spread butter onto bread slices. Place 1 bread slice, butter-side down, in the preheated toaster. Crack egg onto bread slice and break yolk. Top with cheese slice and remaining bread slice, butter-side up.
  3. Close the toaster and place into the coals of the fire. Cook, turning frequently, until bread has browned nicely on both sides, 3 to 4 minutes.
